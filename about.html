About Sunshine

Sunshine is a platform dedicated to providing mental health support and resources to those in need. We believe in promoting wellness, self-care, and the power of community in overcoming challenges.

Our Mission

To create a safe space where individuals can find the support and resources they need for their mental well-being.

Our Vision

Empowering individuals to live healthier and happier lives through mental health awareness, resources, and community support.
